Our Mission

The AI Genomics Webinar series aims to explore the cutting-edge intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and genomics. This monthly series brings together experts from academia, industry, and government agencies to discuss innovative approaches to analyzing and interpreting biological big data.

Our mission is to foster collaboration, knowledge sharing, and advancement in the application of AI and machine learning techniques to genomic research. By showcasing diverse perspectives and groundbreaking methodologies, we seek to accelerate the integration of computational intelligence into genomics, ultimately enhancing our understanding of biological systems and driving innovations in fields such as personalized medicine, agriculture, environmental science, food industry and more.

Our Lab

Our lab, the High Throughput Sequencing Initiative (HTSI), is affiliated with the Institute for Food Safety and Health at Illinois Tech. We provide next generation sequencing (NGS) services using MiSeq and NextSeq 2000. For research, we primarily focus on microbiological research and bioinformatics tool development. Our interests lie in applying AI and machine learning to solve biological problems using big data. For more information about our bioinformatics analysis capabilities, please visit our Galaxy server at hts.iit.edu/galaxy.
